# **Cinematic Wayfinder Cursor Prompt**

## **Core Concept: "The Beacon"**

Create a cursor that feels like a **luminous guide** in a dark, cinematic landscape—a singular point of light that draws the eye and illuminates the path ahead. It should embody the feeling of a **premium flashlight beam** in a luxury vehicle or a **cinematic spotlight** tracking a subject in a dark room.

---

## **Primary Visual Design**

### **The Core Beacon:**
- A **4px diameter perfect circle** of pure, vibrant gold (#FFD700 at 100%)
- **Inner glow:** 8px blur radius with 70% opacity of the same gold
- **Outer aura:** 24px radial gradient fading from gold (20%) to transparent
- **Radiant particles:** 2-3 microscopic gold particles orbiting the center at varying speeds (like electrons around a nucleus)

### **The Path Illumination:**
- A **soft conical light beam** extending 80px ahead of the cursor movement direction
- Beam starts at 30% opacity gold and fades to transparent
- Contains **floating dust particles** (1px × 1px) that catch the light as they pass through
- Beam subtly **bends and distorts** based on movement velocity (like light through glass)

### **Movement Trail:**
- Leaves a **4-6 frame motion trail** that fades from gold to transparent
- Each trail frame is 30% smaller than the previous
- Trail particles have **chromatic aberration** (subtle red/blue edges) when moving quickly
- Creates a **comet-like tail** during rapid movements

---

## **Interactive States & Transformations**

### **1. Default Navigation State:**
- **Beacon:** Gentle pulse animation (scale 1 → 1.1 over 2 seconds, ease-in-out)
- **Beam:** 45° wide cone, subtle light shafts visible in dark interface areas
- **Sound Design Concept:** Barely audible ambient hum (like a high-end projector)
- **Particle Orbit:** Slow, deliberate rotation (90 seconds per full revolution)

### **2. Hover Over Interactive Elements:**
- **Transformation:** Beacon expands to 8px, becomes slightly elliptical (stretches horizontally)
- **Beam:** Widens to 60°, intensifies to 50% opacity
- **Effect:** Projects a **subtle grid pattern** onto the hovered element (like a measurement grid)
- **Particles:** Orbit speed doubles, emit tiny sparkles on collision
- **Text Reveal:** Micro-copy appears above cursor ("SELECT" in 6px elegant serif)

### **3. Project Slit Hover (Special Interaction):**
- **Phase 1 - Discovery:** Beacon morphs into a **keyhole shape**, beam becomes a focused 15° spotlight
- **Phase 2 - Scanning:** Projects **vertical scan lines** down the slit (like a biometric scanner)
- **Phase 3 - Unlocking:** Beacon transforms into a **rotating golden key** silhouette
- **Phase 4 - Revelation:** When slit expands, cursor showers the area with **golden confetti particles** for 300ms

### **4. Click/Drag States:**
- **On Click:** Beacon contracts to 2px, then expands with a **light wave ripple** (3 concentric circles)
- **During Drag:** Beacon becomes a **magnet icon**, beam shows **attraction lines** pulling toward cursor
- **On Release:** Beacon emits a **satisfying "click" light pulse** (brief white flash at 30% opacity)

### **5. Loading/Processing State:**
- Beacon transforms into a **spinning loading ring** (like a gyroscope)
- Ring has **3 rotating gold bands** at different angles
- Projects a **progress bar** on the surface below (10px long, fills over time)
- Emits **processing particles** that flow into the center

---

## **Premium Cinematic Features**

### **Adaptive Behavior:**
- **Speed Sensitivity:** At slow movement: tight 30° beam, high particle count. At fast movement: 90° wide beam, motion blur, chromatic aberration.
- **Interface Context:** Over dark areas: beam visible. Over light areas: beam fades, beacon intensifies.
- **Time of Day:** Beacon warmth shifts subtly (warmer gold at "sunset" hours, cooler at "midnight")

### **Micro-Interactions:**
- **Directional Anticipation:** Beam slightly leans into turns (like a motorcycle leaning)
- **Edge of Screen:** When approaching edges, beacon pulses red twice, beam shows "boundary" pattern
- **Element Boundaries:** When crossing between UI sections, beam briefly fractures into prism colors
- **Idle Animation:** After 10 seconds idle, beacon performs a "lighthouse sweep" (full 360° beam rotation once)

### **Signature Effects:**
1. **Golden Ratio Spiral:** On rare random intervals (1% chance), cursor draws a perfect golden spiral for 2 seconds
2. **Director's Viewfinder:** Double-click creates a temporary cinema frame overlay (2.35:1 aspect ratio)
3. **Path Prediction:** During deliberate movement, shows a 3-frame prediction trail of where you're heading
4. **Focus Breathing:** When over important CTAs, beacon mimics camera focus breathing (gentle pulse with aperture sound)

## **Technical & Atmospheric Details**

### **Visual Refinements:**
- **Lens Flare:** Occasional subtle lens flare when crossing bright interface elements
- **Volumetric Lighting:** Beam illuminates the "grain texture" overlay, making it visible in the light path
- **Heat Haze Effect:** Over intense interactive elements, creates subtle heat distortion above cursor
- **Depth of Field:** Beacon has slight bokeh when over background elements vs sharp over foreground

### **Motion Language:**
- **Primary Movement:** Critically damped spring (stiffness: 400, damping: 25)
- **Secondary Elements:** Trail particles have 50ms delay, beam has 30ms delay
- **Transition Timing:** State changes use your cinematic easing (800ms duration)
- **Micro-Animation:** All animations run at 120fps target

### **Contextual Intelligence:**
- **Over Text:** Beacon shows tiny magnifying glass, beam highlights text with yellow highlighter effect
- **Over Images:** Beacon shows crop icon, beam becomes a color picker sampling colors
- **Over Video:** Beacon shows play icon, beam becomes timeline scrubber
- **Over Code:** Beacon becomes bracket icon, beam syntax-highlights underlying code

## **Implementation Philosophy**

This cursor should never:
- Obscure content or interfere with usability
- Feel sluggish or unresponsive
- Become the main attraction (it's a guide, not the star)
- Break immersion with overly complex effects

This cursor should always:
- Feel like an extension of the user's intention
- Enhance the premium, cinematic atmosphere
- Provide subtle, useful feedback
- Reward attention to detail without demanding it

**The Guiding Principle:** *"A light in the darkness, not just illuminating what is, but revealing what could be."*
