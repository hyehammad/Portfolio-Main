# **Cursor Design Prompt for Cinematic Portfolio**

## **Core Concept: "Quantum Pointer"**

Create an elegant, premium cursor system that feels like a **sophisticated measurement tool from a physics lab** combined with the **precise elegance of a luxury timepiece**. The cursor should be an extension of the portfolio's cinematic, elite aesthetic.

## **Visual Design Direction**

### **Base Cursor Structure:**
```
Primary Layer: A 2×2px perfect square of pure white (#F8F5F0) at 100% opacity
Secondary Layer: A 24px diameter circular wireframe in soft gold (rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.15))
Tertiary Layer: Four microscopic gold points at cardinal positions (N, E, S, W)
```

### **Behavior & Animation Language:**
- **Movement:** 12ms delay with critically damped spring physics (feels precise, not floaty)
- **Transitions:** All state changes use your cinematic easing curve (`cubic-bezier(0.645, 0.045, 0.355, 1)`)
- **Micro-interactions:** Sub-pixel positioning for ultra-smooth motion

## **Context-Aware States**

### **1. Default State (Idle Exploration):**
- **Visual:** Minimalist crosshair (1px strokes) that occasionally emits a single gold particle that drifts downward and fades
- **Behavior:** Subtle breathing animation (scale 1 → 1.05 over 3 seconds)
- **Sound Design Concept:** Complete silence, letting the grain texture audio dominate

### **2. Hover Over Interactive Elements (Buttons, Links):**
- **Transformation:** The square rotates 45° to become a diamond, then scales to 150%
- **Accent:** The gold wireframe briefly intensifies to 30% opacity, then settles to 20%
- **Particle Effect:** Two gold particles spiral outward from the center
- **Timing:** 180ms for full transformation

### **3. Hover Over Project Slits (Your Special Interaction):**
- **Before Expansion (0-300ms):**
  - Cursor becomes a **vertical measurement bar** (40px tall × 4px wide)
  - Displays the project number in a microscopic 6px monospace font
  - Emits a **data stream visualization** (falling binary digits at 5% opacity)

- **During Expansion (300-800ms):**
  - Morphs into a **cinema clapperboard icon** (symbolizing "action" or "scene reveal")
  - Gold wireframe becomes a 32px square with rounded corners
  - Center square pulses gently with the project's accent color

- **After Expansion (800ms+):**
  - Returns to default state but with a **persistent golden aura** around it
  - Leaves faint afterimages (like long exposure photography) when moving over the expanded card

### **4. Drag States (For Your Orbital Gallery):**
- **Visual:** Becomes a **gyroscope/compass** with rotating inner rings
- **Feedback:** Outer ring deforms elliptically based on drag velocity
- **Particles:** Gold dust trails from the drag direction

### **5. Loading/Processing States:**
- **Animation:** Becomes a **progress ring** (quarter circle that fills over time)
- **Detail:** Shows microscopic percentage text (85% opacity)
- **Pulse:** Gentle expansion/contraction at 2Hz frequency

## **Premium Details & Easter Eggs**

### **Hidden Interactions:**
- **Quick Movement:** When mouse accelerates rapidly, cursor **streaks** with motion blur
- **Idle Time:** After 8 seconds of no movement, cursor performs a **watch winding animation** (tiny clockwise rotation)
- **Screen Edges:** When approaching viewport edges, cursor shows **subtle edge resistance** (slows slightly)

### **Time-Based Variations:**
- **Day Cycle:** Gold accent shifts slightly warmer/cooler based on local time
- **Usage Duration:** After 3 minutes of continuous use, reveals a **microscopic patina texture** on the gold elements

### **Project-Specific States:**
- **Over Design Projects:** Cursor gains **grid alignment lines**
- **Over Code Projects:** Shows **syntax highlighting colors** in particles
- **Over Writing Projects:** Briefly becomes a **quill pen icon**

## **Technical Requirements**

### **Performance Considerations:**
- Maximum 3 DOM elements for cursor
- All animations hardware-accelerated
- Frame rate target: 120fps minimum
- Memory usage: < 2MB for cursor system

### **Accessibility:**
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
- High contrast mode fallback
- Screen reader invisible (doesn't interfere with focus)
- Keyboard navigation compatible

### **Browser Support:**
- Primary: Chrome, Safari, Firefox (latest)
- Fallback: Standard cursor on mobile/touch devices
- Graceful degradation on low-performance devices

## **Inspiration References**

1. **Apple Pro Display XDR's reference mode cursor** (precision, calibration)
2. **Leica rangefinder's focus patch** (elegance, accuracy)
3. **Film director's viewfinder** (cinematic framing)
4. **Architect's drafting tools** (precision, minimalism)
5. **Luxury watch complications** (detailed, engineered)

## **Success Metrics**

The cursor should feel:
- **Invisible** when you're not thinking about it
- **Delightful** when you notice the details
- **Premium** in every interaction
- **Purposeful** in its design
- **Memorable** as part of the overall experience

---

**Final Note:** This cursor shouldn't shout for attention but should reward close observation. It's the Swiss watch of cursors—understated excellence that reveals its quality through use, not appearance.
