# **Cursor: The Cinematic Light Pen**

## **Concept: "The Director's Light Pointer"**

Create a cursor that feels like a **film director's light pointer used during editing sessions**—a precise, professional tool that highlights, selects, and directs attention with cinematic flair.

---

## **Core Design: Light Pen & Exposure Trails**

### **Primary Elements:**

**The Pen Tip (Focus Point):**
- **Shape:** Perfect circle with **four subtle spikes** at cardinal points (like a compass rose or lens iris)
- **Size:** 6px diameter when stationary, expands contextually
- **Color:** **Cine-grade white** (#FFFFFF) core with **golden corona** (#FFD700) at edges
- **Effect:** **Subtle anamorphic lens flare** with **horizontal streaks** when moving quickly
- **Texture:** **Crisp, sharp edges** with **1px glowing outline** that shifts between your accent colors

**The Light Beam (Directional Guide):**
- **Form:** A **conical light beam** extending 60px ahead of movement direction
- **Quality:** **Volumetric lighting** with visible **dust particles** in the beam
- **Gradient:** Starts at **100% opacity white**, fades through **gold**, ends in **transparent**
- **Behavior:** **Bends slightly** toward interactive elements (magnetic attraction)
- **Dynamic:** Width adjusts based on movement speed (narrow at rest, wide when moving fast)

**The Exposure Trail (Cinematic Memory):**
- **Effect:** Leaves a **4-frame motion trail** that looks like **long exposure photography**
- **Composition:** Each frame is **30% transparent** and shows **chromatic aberration** (red/blue edges)
- **Duration:** Trail persists for **300ms**, fading gracefully
- **Special:** When changing direction abruptly, creates **light streak smears** (like motion blur)

---

## **Interactive States & Transformations**

### **1. Default Navigation (Exploring):**
- **Pen Tip:** Gentle **focus breathing** (pulse from 6px to 7px over 2 seconds)
- **Beam:** 45° wide, **subtle scan lines** moving outward (like projector beam)
- **Sound Concept:** Almost inaudible **35mm film through gate** whir
- **Atmosphere:** Barely visible **light particles** float into beam, illuminate, fade

### **2. Hover Over Interactive Elements:**
- **Transformation:** Pen tip becomes that element's **miniature icon** (scaled to 12px)
  - Buttons → Arrow or Hand icon
  - Links → Chain link icon
  - Images → Magnifying glass
  - Text → I-beam cursor
- **Beam:** Contracts to **30° spotlight**, intensifies to **150% brightness**
- **Effect:** Projects **subtle grid** or **alignment guides** onto element
- **Reveal:** Element gets **edge glow** in beam's color

### **3. Project Slit Hover (Special Sequence):**
**Phase 1 - Detection (0-100ms):**
- Pen tip becomes **clapperboard icon**, beam becomes **vertical scanner**
- Projects **film reel sprocket holes** onto slit

**Phase 2 - Selection (100-300ms):**
- Transforms into **red recording dot** (like camera tally light)
- Beam becomes **focus pull rings** (concentric circles measuring distance)

**Phase 3 - Expansion (300-600ms):**
- Becomes **zooming lens icon** with aperture blades visible
- Beam **widens to reveal** the expanding card

**Phase 4 - Interaction (600ms+):**
- Settles as **director's viewfinder** with aspect ratio overlay
- Beam highlights specific details on expanded card

### **4. Form Field Interaction:**
- **Focus:** Pen tip becomes **blinking I-beam** made of light
- **Typing:** Each keystroke emits **tiny light particle** that floats into text
- **Validation:** **Green check** or **red X** light burst
- **Success:** **Golden confetti burst** from pen tip

### **5. Drag/Selection States:**
- **Visual:** Pen tip becomes **two crossed arrows** (move icon)
- **Beam:** Becomes **tape measure** extending from origin point
- **Trail:** Leaves **solid light trail** that persists until release
- **Release:** Trail **snaps into selection box** with satisfying light flash

### **6. Loading/Processing:**
- Pen tip becomes **spinning film reel** (3 frames visible)
- Beam becomes **progress bar** on surface below
- Emits **processing particles** that flow into center

## **Cinematic Special Features**

### **Adaptive Intelligence:**
- **Speed:** Slow movement = **tight beam, high particle count**. Fast movement = **wide beam, motion blur, chromatic aberration**
- **Context:** Over dark areas = **beam visible, particles illuminated**. Over light areas = **beam invisible, pen tip intensifies**
- **Time:** Golden hour = **warm amber tones**. Night = **cool blue with star-like particles**

### **Signature Effects:**
1. **Director's Cut:** Double-click anywhere creates **temporary aspect ratio overlay** (2.35:1 cinemascope bars)
2. **Continuity:** When moving between related elements, draws **light connection line** between them
3. **Frame Perfect:** Holding Shift makes cursor **snap to grid lines** with magnetic pull
4. **Behind the Scenes:** Right-click reveals **wireframe overlay** of page structure

### **Scene-Specific Behaviors:**
- **Hero Section:** Cursor has **gentle fog effect** in beam (atmospheric)
- **About Page:** Shows **timeline markers** when hovering over dates
- **Skills Section:** Projects **skill measurement rings** (like radar)
- **Projects Gallery:** Becomes **orbital navigation tool** with rotation guides
- **Contact Section:** Transforms into **light pen for signing** (calligraphy effect)

## **Premium Details & Easter Eggs**

### **Hidden Interactions:**
- **Quick Diagonal Movement:** Creates **lightning bolt trail** that lingers briefly
- **Circular Motion:** Generates **spiral vortex** effect

- **Screen Corners:** When hitting edges, shows **"bounce"** with light splash

### **Production Quality Indicators:**
- **Frame Rate Monitor:** When moving in perfect circles, shows **fps counter** (hidden by default)
- **Color Temperature:** Changes subtly based on section's dominant color
- **Focus Distance:** Shows **virtual depth measurement** when moving toward/away from elements
- **Light Meter:** When over images, shows **exposure value** in beam

### **Narrative Elements:**
- **First Visit:** Cursor starts **dim**, gains **brightness** as user explores
- **Completion:** After viewing all sections, cursor gains **golden laurel wreath** around tip
- **Return Visits:** Remembers **frequently used paths**, pre-illuminates them slightly

## **Technical Execution Notes**

### **Performance Priority:**
- Maximum of **5 DOM elements** for cursor system
- All animations **GPU accelerated** (transform and opacity only)
- **60fps minimum**, 120fps target on capable devices
- **Progressive enhancement** - simpler version for mobile/tablet

### **Accessibility:**
- **High contrast mode:** Becomes **solid black circle** with **white outline**
- **Reduced motion:** Removes trail, simplifies to basic pointer
- **Keyboard navigation:** Shows **focus ring** around cursor position
- **Screen readers:** Completely hidden from assistive tech

### **Browser Compatibility:**
- **Primary:** Full experience on Chrome, Safari, Firefox
- **Fallback:** Standard system cursor on IE/Edge Legacy
- **Mobile:** Simple circle that follows touch (no beam/trail)
